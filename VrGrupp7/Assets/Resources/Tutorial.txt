Hello! Welcome to you new job!
On the screen to the left you will get an order for a specific potion.
The dispensers to the right will give you diferent kinds of liquid.
You Should mix the liquid inside the bottles to make the desired potion.
Some potions need a little extra that you can get from the Gems from the stones on the table behind you.
To extract the stones use the blowtorch and hammer to remove the rock.
Use the scanner on the table to see the contents of a potion.
