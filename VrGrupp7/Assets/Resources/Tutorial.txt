On the screen to the left you will get an order for a specific potion.
The potion does not have to be exactly the potency specified.
To create potions you will need to extract gems from the stones on the table behind you.
Extract the gems by using the hammer and blowtorch. Then using the mortar and pestle to crush the gems.
Add the dust and water to a bottle and then mix it by shaking.
Use the scanner on the table to see the contents of a potion.
