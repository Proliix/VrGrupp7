On the screen to the left you will get an order for a specific potion.
The potion does not have to be exactly the potency specified.
To create potions you will need to extract gems from stones.
Extract the gems by using the hammer and blowtorch.
Then use the mortar and pestle to crush the gems into a powder.
Add the powder to a bottle. Then put some water in it. 
You can get water at the table in the far right corner behind you.
Now shake the bottle to mix it. use the scanner thats on the table to see the contents of a potion.
You can use the blowtorch on the gems to see what effect it has by its changeing color.
